Assignment 3: Minecraft Factory Planner: Sleepy Town needs Books

Part 1

Your first order for Sleepy Town was so successful, that they are coming back to get more items made by you and your lumberjack team. Sleepy Town needs 20 Books. Letâ€™s use the Minecraft Factory Planner to see just what materials you will need to fulfill this order.

1.  In the Planner tool find the Books in the Materials Tab, under Craftable filter. Drag and drop this item onto the Planner.

2. The recipe for Books is Leather, Paper and Sugar Cane. In the Materials Tab, under the Resource and Craftable filter, find Leather, Paper and Sugar Cane. Drag them on the Planner.

3. Connect Leather and Paper to the Book block and the Sugar Cane to Paper in the planner.

Part 2:

1. To estimate the number, we need to add an output block for Books to the planner. Find the Books in the materials tab, under Output and drag it to the Planner.

2. Connect the Books recipe to the input of the Output block you just added to the Planner

3. Right click on the Output the block and select “focus”

4. Now that you have the Output focused, you can look to see how many Books can be made every hour by click on the “Graph” button in the Data Window.

5. Now lets see how many Leather and Paper are needed to generate 20 Books every hour. Adjust the number in the Sugar Cane, Leather and Paper blocks until you see 20 in the Books block.

Awesome! Now we can go back to Tiny Town and fulfill this order, we need know exactly what we need to build 20 Books for Sleepy Town!
