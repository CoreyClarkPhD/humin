Assignment 1: Minecraft Lumberjack Tycoon.

This assignment will get you setup in Minecraft and link your account with this course. You will get familiarized with the Tiny Town world and meet your guides Pepper Pots and Tony Spark. They will guide you through filling your first production order of Planks for the lumberjack team. Once complete, you will have created your first lumbermill and produced your very first order for Sleepy Town.

1. Login to your Minecraft EDU client using your SMU ID at the following IP address: mineplex.humingamelab.com

2. Once the game has loaded, press t or / to open the command line. Enter the following text into the command line: /link . As an example, if your SMU ID is 123456, the command would look like:
 /link 123456
This links your Minecraft account with your Canvas account.

3. Once you have entered the game and linked your account, go meet up with Pepper Pots and work through the task he presents you.

4. Once you complete the task with Pepper Pots, she will direct you over to Tony Spark to complete your first order Sleepy Town. Complete all of the task with Tony Spark, then come back here to complete Assignment 2.
