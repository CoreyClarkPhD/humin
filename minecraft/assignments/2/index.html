Assignment 2: Minecraft Factory Planner.

Now that have you completed your first order for Sleepy Town, we need to figure out how many Planks can be made every day for Sleep Town. This assignment will walk you through using the Minecraft Factory Planner to figure out just that!

1. In the Planner tool find the Planks block in your Minecraft Inventory window and drag it to the Planner window.

2. Now the Minecraft block shows how many Planks are being created every hour in Tiny Town! You can press the refresh button on your Minecraft inventory window to update the current inventory. Even while you are here planning out your next big order for Sleep Town, your Tiny Town lumber mill is still producing Planks for you!
